Lasagna

Description

This is a delicious classic lasagna with layers of pasta, cheese, and meat sauce.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. Prepare the meat sauce by cooking ground beef with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes, then let cool before serving.
