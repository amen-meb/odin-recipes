Salad

Description

This is a fresh and healthy salad with a mix of greens, vegetables, and a light vinaigrette dressing.

Ingredients

  • Mixed greens (lettuce, spinach, arugula)
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Cucumber slices
  • Red onion
  • Feta cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Wash and dry the mixed greens.
  2. Halve the cherry tomatoes and slice the cucumber and red onion.
  3. In a large bowl, combine the mixed greens, cherry tomatoes, cucumber slices, and red onion.
  4. Crumble feta cheese on top of the salad.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, balsamic vinegar, salt, and pepper to make the vinaigrette dressing.
  6. Drizzle the dressing over the salad and toss to combine before serving.
